About TinBEANS


Tradition since 2018

The TinBEANS was founded in Balulang by Ms. Christine B. Sevilla. We are serving coffee you can enjoy.
Step into our cozy coffee shop, where the aroma of freshly brewed coffee dances in the air, welcoming you to indulge in a moment of tranquility. With warm, rustic decor and soft ambient lighting, our space invites you to unwind and savor your favorite brew. Whether you're seeking a rich espresso, a creamy latte, or a fragrant herbal tea, our skilled baristas craft each beverage with precision and care. Sink into plush armchairs or find inspiration at communal tables while you savor artisanal pastries and savory snacks. Whether you're catching up with friends, diving into a good book, or simply enjoying a quiet moment of reflection, our coffee shop is your sanctuary in the bustling cityscape.
